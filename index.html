Details About Terrance:

  • Series: Angry Birds by Rovio
  • Species: Red Bird
  • Gender: Male
  • Color: Maroon/Dark Red
  • First Appearance: Angry Birds (2011 update introducing Big Brother Bird)
  • Personality: Grim, silent, and loyal
  • Ability: Technically none, other than dealing more destruction than other birds
  • TV Show: Appears in Angry Birds Toons
  • Voice Actor: Flula Borg voiced him in the 1st and 2nd Angry Birds Movies
  • Family: Despite the early depiction of the "Big Brother Bird", he is NOT Red's brother
  • Strength: He is the strongest bird, apart from special event birds.
